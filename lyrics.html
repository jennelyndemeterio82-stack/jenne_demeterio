Song Details:

  • Artist: Taylor Swift
  • Album: Taylor Swift (debut album)
  • Release Date: February 20, 2007
  • Genre: Country Pop
  • Chart Performance: Reached #2 on the billboard hot Country songs and #13 on the Billboard hot 100

    • Inspiration and Meaning

  • Real-life subject: The song is about Swift's unrequited love for a classmate named hardwick
  • The Scenario: Swift was friends with Drew, who would confide in her about the girl he liked and ask for her advice, unaware of her feelings
  • The Narrator's Role: Swift's narrator is the "to whom Drew would go for advice, pretending to be supportive while hiding her own heartbreak

    • Key Themes

  • Unrequited Love: The central theme is loving someone who doesn't reciprocate those feelings
  • Heartbreak and Yearning: The lyrics convey the pain and longing of unfulfilled affection
  • Insecurity: The narrator's insecurities are evident in lines like " I bet she's beautiful" and " Can he tell that Ican't breathe?"

    • Music Video

  • Plot: The video depicts scenes of Swift and Drew at school and shows the moment she sees him with his girlfriend, leading to her heartbroken departure
  • Actor: Tyler Hilton, an actor and musician, portrays Drew in the video
